# Government Links

## Know Mobile Connections in Your Name

Check the number of mobile connections issued in your name by logging in with your mobile number.

**Service**: Sanchar Saathi - Telecom Analytics for Fraud Management and Consumer Protection

https://tafcop.sancharsaathi.gov.in/telecomUser/

---

*This service helps you identify unauthorized mobile connections that may have been fraudulently registered in your name.*
